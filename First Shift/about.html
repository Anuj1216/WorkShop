About Us




Mahendra Morang Adarsh Multiple Campus, located in Biratnagar, Nepal, is a prominent constituent campus of Tribhuvan University in Province No. 1. Established in 1955 as 'Morang Intermediate College,' it has a rich history of academic excellence It Offers a wide range of courses at both undergraduated and postgraduate levels These include Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Tachnology(BSc.CSIT), Bachelor of Information Technology(BIT), Bachelor of Arts(BA), Master of Arts(MA)in English, Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Public Administration (BPA), Ph.D. in Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Micro-Biology, and Environmental Science. The campus provides scholarships based on academic performance and supports various disciplines such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Public Administration, Business Studies, and English at different academic levels.

Our Faculties

Institute of Science and Technology The Institute of Science and Technology is a higher education institution that specializes in offering programs and courses related to science and technology. It is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation in various scientific and technological fields.

Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences The Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences is an academic division within a university or college that encompasses a wide range of disciplines related to human culture, society, and individual behavior. It typically offers undergraduate and graduate programs in subjects such as literature, history, philosophy, sociology, psychology, anthropology, linguistics, communication studies, political science, and economics, among others.

Faculty of Management The Faculty of Management is responsible for providing education and training in various areas of business administration, leadership, entrepreneurship, finance, marketing, human resources, operations, and other related fields. It aims to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and competencies necessary to succeed in the business world and assume managerial roles in organizations.

Faculty of Education Faculty of Education Home Faculties Faculty of Education The faculty of Education is a vital component of any higher education institution, as it plays a crucial role in equipping future educators with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in their chosen profession. Within this faculty, students undergo rigorous academic training guided by experienced teachers and scholars, who provide them with practical insights and theoretical frameworks for effective teaching practices.
